Daily Wellness Tips

Small changes that can make a big difference in your overall well-being

Hydration First

Start your day with a glass of water before coffee or tea to rehydrate after sleep.

Movement Breaks

Take a 5-minute walk every hour to improve circulation and reduce sedentary time.

Digital Sunset

Avoid screens 1 hour before bed to improve sleep quality and melatonin production.

Rainbow Plate

Include at least 3 different colored vegetables in each meal for diverse nutrients.

Mindful Moments

Practice 3 minutes of deep breathing when stressed to activate relaxation response.

Gratitude Practice

Write down 3 things you're grateful for each day to boost mental well-being.
